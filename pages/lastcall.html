Last Call Trivia

The Brief

Last Call Trivia, a nationwide pub quiz league, needed a new site in preparation of the start of the spring 2017 trivia season. They wanted a modern, responsive site that displays important league information clearly, while remaining consistent with their recent brand redesign.

My role in the project was to design the layout of the site based on their existing branding guidelines, and to assist the frontend developer with implementing this design.

The Process

From using the old site for many trivia seasons, the client already had a pretty clear idea of how they wanted the navigation to look for the new one. My focus was on taking these ideas and bringing them to life in a way that captured the distinct look and feel of their branding for print materials.
After the designs were finalized, my role shifted to one of front-end development; working with the rest of the team to build a library of css components to easily apply the visual language of the designs to the rest of the website.
