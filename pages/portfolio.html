Password Reset Mobile App

A project taken to strengthen my own UX skills, by envisioning a mobile app to solve a common problem at a previous workplace. Password lockouts were common, but the tools that existed were either not accessible or underutilized due to their difficulty of access. This project aims to solve these issues with an app that is always ready at hand and easy for anyone to use.
